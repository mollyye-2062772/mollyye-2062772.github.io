Rankings

UW among best in world for computer science and engineering

The UW moved up five spots to No. 16 in computer science in the Times Higher Education World University Rankings by Subject 2022, making it third among U.S. public universities. Engineering, meanwhile, moved up seven places from last year.

Coronavirus

WA Notify app alerts you to potential COVID-19 exposure

Help protect yourself and your loved ones by downloading WA Notify. It’s a smartphone app that lets you know when you’ve potentially been exposed to COVID-19. WA Notify is free, completely private, and does not track your activity or identity.

UW and the Community

Get to know the UW campus with Indigenous Walking Tour

During his senior year, Owen Oliver (Quinault Indian Nation and Isleta Pueblo) created a walking tour of UW’s Seattle campus, highlighting the Indigenous presence on campus, including the Burke Museum, which is the first stop on the tour.
